About

Founded in 2021, Superwomen Pakistan has been Bridging the Gap ever since:  Pakistan's 1st Health and Hygiene Initiative for Women. A proactive approach to empower women's health and hygiene in a country where gender inequality and a lack of access to female hygiene products are persistent issues. Therefore, in collaboration with DigiTrends Ventures, Superwomen Pakistan bridges the gap by providing women with education, and quality self-care products, allowing women to better care for their hygiene and health.

DigiTrends Ventures
Empowers 

Superwomen Pakistan to Revolutionize Female Hygiene with Cutting-Edge E-commerce Mobile App

DigiTrends Ventures has assisted Superwomen Pakistan in realizing its vision of empowering women with products and information about female hygiene by incubating this innovative startup and providing cutting-edge technology in a world where startups face numerous challenges in reaching their goals and full potential.

Superwomen Pakistan was able to develop a mobile e-commerce application with the assistance of DigiTrends Ventures, allowing them to reach a large audience and effectively communicate their mission.
